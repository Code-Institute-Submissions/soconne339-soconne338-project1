What is a lifehack?

A lifehack is a unique technique or strategy that increases productivity and efficiency. Most of us have completed lifehacks without realising it. Perhaps you're someone who’s found an interesting way to pour your milk for your tea without causing any splashes!? Or you’ve figured out a way to stop cables from getting untidy? You could be carrying out lifehacks and not even realise it.
This site will demonstrate some of the common lifehacks and if you learn something new, that’s even better!

How to use Google to search inside of any website?

Google is a great tool for eearching for many different queries but did you know that you can also use Google to search for information inside any website. This can be done be using the site: operator.
For example, lets say I wanted to search for information related to football inside of the website rte.ie. To do this I would use site:rte.ie football … and Google would provide me all pages on rte.ie which are connected to the word football

How to reopen an accidentally closed web browser window or tab?

There's nothing worse than when you find a really good web page, only to discover you've gone and closed it! Never fear. You can reopen a closed web page by clicking on Ctrl + Shift + T.
THis can be done for more than 1 website you have recently closed down

Cleaning Hacks

These hacks will give you a helping hand when it comes to cleaning

How to clean a carpet which has been stained with red wine?

Spilling red wine on a carpet is enough to make even the toughest of men cry. Fortunately there is a solution. Clear vodka is really helpful for getting red wine stains out of carpets. After blotting the stain, use the vodka to break it up. After some intense rubbing, you are left with a carpet that is completely stain free!

How to get your microwave looking as good as new?

Microwaves have a habit of picking up disgusting food stains and grease. Yuck! Here is a great tip to give your microwave looking its best.
Add some lemon juice or vinegar to a cup and set your microwave on 5-10 mins. Afterwards you can simply wipe away the stubburn grease and your microwave will look and smell fresh.

How to remove stubborn water stains on your tap?

To remove stubborn water stains on your taps, grab a lemon and cut it in half. Take one of the halfs of the lemon and rub vigourously on the stained area. Wash off with clean water and you are left with super shiny taps.

How to get your windows looking brand new?

If your windows are not looking their shiniest then grab an empty spray bottle and add white vinegar, water and some drops of essential oil. give the spray bottle a good shake Creating your own homemade window cleaner is not only more economic but is also free from harsh chemicals.

  2. Add your can to the bowl and cover with the ice
